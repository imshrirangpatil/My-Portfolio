Oregon State University (July 2024 - June 2025)

  • This project, funded by the 2024 Transdisciplinary Research Seed Fund Program, aims to enhance human-machine and human-human interactions through an AI companion that recognizes users by voice, analyzes speech intent and sentiment, and provides real-time feedback.
